Addressing the Excessive Consumption of Energy by Tech Infrastructure

The exponential growth of technology has led to a surge in energy demand across various sectors. Data centers, which store and process vast amounts of information, are one of the most energy-intensive components of tech infrastructure. Additionally, the manufacturing and disposal of electronic devices contribute to environmental degradation and energy consumption.

We at Greenbyte explore the impact of excess energy consumption by tech sector and potential solutions towards a sustainable future.
